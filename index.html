About Me

I'm a Data Scientist

Hi, I’m Pratishtha, a passionate Data Scientist with a deep interest in the inner workings of ML systems. Currently pursuing my Master’s in Data Science at UCSD, I’m on the verge of graduation and actively seeking opportunities to apply my skills in the real world. My journey in machine learning began with a foundation in CUDA, which has fueled my fascination with training and scaling large language models (LLMs). I’ve honed my expertise in optimizing transformer models and GEMM operations on both GPUs and CPUs. My recent endeavors include improving KV cache utilization for multi-round conversations in LLMs by implementing a priority-based eviction policy. I’m also a proud member of the Data Science Graduate Association at UCSD, where I’ve been collaborating on introducing Generative AI to my peers. One of my key interests is making AI available for all, small business, people with fewer GPUs. Additionally, I enjoy sharing research articles and insights to help others understand the complexities of ML systems. As I look toward the future, I’m eager to contribute to cutting-edge projects in machine learning, leveraging my skills to drive innovation. Whether it’s through research, development, or optimization, I’m dedicated to pushing the boundaries of what’s possible in AI.
